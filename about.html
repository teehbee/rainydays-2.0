ABOUT US

Ever since the RainyDays founder RainyDays Rainn William Jacket invented the rain jacket in 1952 we have provided the population with the best rain garments for an active lifestyle. From the humble beginnings at the small factory in the worlds rain-capitol, Bergen, to the fanciest shops on 5th avenue in New York our story is rich and full of tragedy and joy.

As the world was not ready for lightweight and breathable rain gear, this was sold in illegal markets around the world, before the world leaders finally realized that being protected from the rain was a human right.

As rich as our story is this is still only the beginning. We are always striving to get further and better, and we want you to be a part of that journey!
